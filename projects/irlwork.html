IRL work

This page details non-hobby work I've been formally employed for.

STUDIO//LYNFLO (2023 - present)

In late 2023 I started working with STUDIO//LYNFLO offering general development assistance with their upcoming game DRAGONFRUIT: a mother's love, powered by Godot Engine. Here is just one example of what I've worked on: a dynamic shadow system that switches between realtime shadow casting and a static "directionless" shadow sprite based on distance and intensity of surrounding lights.

GalaxyTrail, LLC (2022 - present)

In late 2022 I was hired by GalaxyTrail under contract to work on various projects, with work centered around prototyping core game systems, integration of assets, and implementation of certain graphical effects. The first to be announced is Chrono Gear: Warden of Time, a hololive game being published under the holo Indie label, set to release in 2025.

John Thurman Field/Modesto Nuts Professional Baseball (2018 - 2019, 2023 - 2024)

(2018-19 as Vincent/Amy Rossow)

For over three seasons, I worked on the production team for a Minor League Baseball stadium, covering needs such as game video camera work, DJing/managing the sound system, directing game cameras, and live replays/footage archival.
